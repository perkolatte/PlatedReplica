WHAT'S IN THE PLATED BOX

We're choosy about what goes into your box. From seasonal produce and meats raised without antibiotics, to simple step-by-step recipe cards and leak-proof packaging; every detail has been considered to ensure an exceptional culinary experience.

RECIPE CARD

From our chef's inspiration behind the meal to insider plating tips, our recipe cards take you on a step-by-step journey through the culinary experience.

INGREDIENTS

We exclusively source meats raised without antibiotics, sustainable seafood, and fresh produce. Everything arrives pre-portioned to minimize food waste and prep time.

THE BOX

Don't worry if you're not home—our 100% recyclable, refrigerated box keeps ingredients fresh at your door for up to 24 hours after the expected delivery date.
